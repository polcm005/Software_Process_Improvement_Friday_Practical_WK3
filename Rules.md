 # Rules

## Players
### No of Players: 4
### Every player has initial victory points: 25

## Winning Goal
### First player to get 50 victory points wins the game.

## Order of Turns
### Players take turns in a clockwise order.
### Roll 2 six-sided dice on every turn

## Dice Rules
### If total > 6, player can take a turn action.
### If total < 6, player loses the action turn.

## Board Movement
- A player moves based on the dice roll.
- Once the player moves, players resolves the effect of the tile they land on rolling dice.

## Types of Tiles On Board
### Bad Tiles
- Bad tiles have negative effects.
- **Penalty**: Player gets a -2 penalty on next roll.
- **Ambush**: Player loses 2 victory points.
- **Move backwards**: Player must move back 1-3 spaces.

#### Good Tiles
- Good tiles give rewards.
- **Steal points**: Roll one die and steal that many victory points from another player.
- **Gain Points**: Roll one die and gain that many victory points.

### Mini Game Questionnaire
- Players draw a card from question deck.
- They have 15 seconds to answer.
